Carbohydrates

Primary energy source for workouts and recovery

Vegetarian
  • Whole wheat roti/chapati
  • Brown rice
  • Oats & porridge
  • Millets (ragi, jowar, bajra)
  • Potatoes & sweet potatoes
  • Fruits (banana, mango)
Non-Vegetarian
  • Pair with whole grains
  • Rice with chicken/fish
  • Roti with meat curries
  • Quinoa with eggs
  • Sweet potato with fish

Proteins

Essential for muscle repair and growth

Vegetarian
  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Soya chunks & tofu
  • Lentils & dals
  • Chickpeas (chana)
  • Kidney beans (rajma)
  • Milk & curd (dahi)
Non-Vegetarian
  • Chicken breast
  • Eggs & egg whites
  • Fish (rohu, katla)
  • Lean mutton/goat
  • Prawns & shrimp
  • Turkey breast

Healthy Fats

For hormone balance and satiety

Vegetarian
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • Seeds (chia, flax, sesame)
  • Avocado
  • Cold-pressed oils
  • Coconut (raw, oil)
  • Ghee (in moderation)
Non-Vegetarian
  • Fatty fish (sardines)
  • Fish oil supplements
  • Egg yolks
  • Lean animal fats
